简介 / Introduction

我关注人与环境之间的关系，偏好具有电影感、情绪氛围与叙事感的摄影表达。
My work explores the relationship between people and environment, with a focus on cinematic atmosphere and narrative imagery.

通过摄影记录人与世界的相遇。在人像、城市与旅行的场景中，我尝试寻找那些安静却具有力量的瞬间。
Through photography I document encounters between people and the world. Across portraits, cities, and travel, I look for quiet moments that still carry emotional weight.

关于 / About

我是一名摄影创作者，关注人与城市空间之间的关系，偏好电影感构图、安静情绪与具有叙事感的画面。 I am a photographer interested in the relationship between people and urban space, with a focus on cinematic composition and quiet emotional atmosphere.

作品分类包含人像、活动、人文风光、实验与食物，这些内容共同构成一个更混合、更开放的视觉系统。 The categories include portrait, events, humanistic landscape, experimental work, and food, forming a more mixed and open visual system.
